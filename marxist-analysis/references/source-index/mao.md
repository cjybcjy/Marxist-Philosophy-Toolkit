# Mao Source Index

Use this file when the answer needs author-only grounding for Mao. Treat the texts as method anchors for contradiction, investigation, practice, mass line, strategy, and construction-stage contradictions.

## Inclusion Rule

Include only Mao's own articles, reports, speeches, talks, instructions, and collected works. Exclude biographies, memoirs, business-strategy reinterpretations, and later summaries unless explicitly requested as later interpretation.

## Core Text Map

| Text cluster | Year | Use it for | Method granularity | Boundary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `Analysis of the Classes in Chinese Society` | 1925 | Class investigation, alliance, friend/enemy judgment | Classify forces by interests and capacity in the concrete struggle | Do not copy historical class names into today without investigation |
| `Report on an Investigation of the Peasant Movement in Hunan` | 1927 | Mass movement, peasant politics, direct investigation | Let field investigation correct elite or bookish assumptions | Do not equate every mass impulse with correct line |
| `Why Is It That Red Political Power Can Exist in China?`; `A Single Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire` | 1928-1930 | Base areas, weak-to-strong route, uneven development | Find the small area where conditions allow survival and expansion | Do not romanticize weakness or avoid stage evidence |
| `Oppose Book Worship` | 1930 | Investigation, anti-dogmatism, decision before speech | Refuse conclusions without concrete investigation | Do not turn anti-book worship into anti-theory |
| `Problems of Strategy in China's Revolutionary War` | 1936 | Military strategy, active defense, concentration | Match tactics to enemy, terrain, forces, and stage | Do not use military formulas as business metaphors without translation |
| `On Practice` | 1937 | Knowledge-practice cycle, experience, verification | Move from practice to knowledge and back to tested practice | Do not call any result proof without defining the feedback loop |
| `On Contradiction` | 1937 | Main contradiction, principal aspect, transformation | Identify the contradiction whose movement changes the whole situation | Do not label every visible problem the main contradiction |
| `On Protracted War` | 1938 | Stage analysis, strategic patience, weak-to-strong transformation | Analyze strength/weakness dynamically through time | Do not use delay as strategy when conditions are deteriorating |
| `Problems of War and Strategy` | 1938 | Political power, armed struggle, strategy | Tie strategy to control of power and organization | Do not transfer emergency war logic to ordinary disputes |
| `The Chinese Revolution and the Chinese Communist Party`; `On New Democracy` | 1939-1940 | Revolution stage, united front, Chinese conditions | Define stage by social structure and historical task | Do not hide contradictions under localization |
| `Reform Our Study`; `Rectify the Party's Style of Work` | 1941-1942 | Style of study, anti-subjectivism, party rectification | Correct style by linking theory, history, and present investigation | Do not turn rectification into personal moral scolding |
| `Talks at the Yenan Forum on Literature and Art` | 1942 | Ideology, culture, audience, political line | Ask whom culture serves and through what forms | Do not reduce all cultural judgment to propaganda utility |
| `Some Questions Concerning Methods of Leadership` | 1943 | Mass line, leadership method, synthesis and return | From masses to masses: gather, synthesize, return, test | Do not equate polling or popularity with mass line |
| `On Coalition Government` | 1945 | United front, state vision, political coalition | Combine alliance, independence, and stage task | Do not erase conflict inside a coalition |
| `On the People's Democratic Dictatorship` | 1949 | State form, class alliance, transition | Define who holds power and why under a new state form | Do not treat it as a generic governance slogan |
| `On the Ten Major Relationships` | 1956 | Construction-stage contradictions, balancing relations | Handle development by balancing central/local, industry/agriculture, state/society tensions | Do not reduce construction policy to one contradiction |
| `On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People` | 1957 | Antagonistic vs non-antagonistic contradictions, governance | Choose handling method by contradiction type | Do not turn all internal contradictions into enemy contradictions |

## Method Extraction Tests

- Cross-domain reproduction: contradiction, investigation, and practice recur across philosophy, class, military, party, and construction texts.
- Generativity: the method produces a new investigation plan or stage judgment, not merely a famous phrase.
- Historical-task test: identify whether the text addresses revolutionary survival, united front, party rectification, state founding, or socialist construction.
- Boundary test: distinguish struggle against enemies from handling contradictions among the people.

## Case Calibration

| Case | Source anchor | What it calibrates |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jinggangshan route after failed urban uprisings | `Why Is It That Red Political Power Can Exist in China?`; `A Single Spark Can Start a Prairie Fire` | Base-area strategy, weak-side survival, uneven development |
| Anti-Japanese War stage analysis | `On Protracted War` | Strategic defense, stalemate, counter-offensive, and time as a material factor |
| Anti-Japanese united front | `On Coalition Government`; class-analysis writings | Unite, struggle, and maintain independence under a larger main contradiction |
| Yan'an rectification | `Reform Our Study`; `Rectify the Party's Style of Work` | Correcting style through investigation, history, and practice |
| Socialist construction balancing | `On the Ten Major Relationships`; `On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People` | Handling non-antagonistic contradictions and development tradeoffs |
