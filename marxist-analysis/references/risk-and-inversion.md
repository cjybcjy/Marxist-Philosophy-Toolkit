# Risk And Inversion

Use this file before recommending action, experiments, reform, strategy, investment-style judgment, career moves, or organizational changes.

## Core Rule

```text
Before asking how this succeeds, ask how it fails and who pays.
```

A practice test is not responsible unless its downside, reversibility, and feedback owner are clear.

## Inversion Questions

Ask these before giving a route:

1. How could this plan fail?
2. What would make the failure irreversible?
3. What is the maximum tolerable loss?
4. Who bears the cost if the judgment is wrong?
5. Who benefits if the plan looks successful but harms the system?
6. Which metric can be gamed?
7. What should be removed before adding a new action?
8. What evidence would make us stop?

## Risk Map

| Risk | Marxist Use |
| --- | --- |
| Downside | Name who bears material cost, not only abstract risk |
| Reversibility | Prefer small tests where wrong judgment can be corrected |
| Skin in the game | Check whether decision-maker, executor, and affected people share consequences |
| Incentive distortion | Identify rewards that make false reports or harmful behavior rational |
| Fragility | Ask whether the system needs stable conditions to survive |
| Asymmetry | Seek low-downside evidence gathering before high-downside commitment |
| Via negativa | Remove harmful constraints before adding complex interventions |

## Barbell Practice Pattern

When stakes are high:

```text
保护底线：
- 保留现金流、时间、健康、关系、组织合法性或学习能力。

小额试错：
- 用可承受损失换真实反馈。

禁止 all-in：
- 除非失败不会摧毁继续实践的能力。
```

## High-Stakes Career Gate

Use this before advising on resignation, full-time entrepreneurship, all-in commitments, major debt, public commitments, relocation, or irreversible career moves.

Do not frame the issue as courage versus fear. Frame it as whether the user's reproduction conditions can survive a wrong judgment.

```text
现实约束：
- cash runway:
- skill/product capability:
- market/user evidence:
- family or social reproduction cost:
- organization/support system:
- information gaps:

all-in 禁止线：
- 如果失败会切断现金流、健康、关系、居住、学习能力或下一轮试错能力，不建议 all-in。

staged experiment:
- 阶段 1：保留底线，用业余/兼职/请假/小额预算验证需求。
- 阶段 2：只有在用户证据和交付能力同时达标后，扩大时间和资金投入。
- 阶段 3：只有在下行可承受且替代现金流明确后，考虑全职。

停止条件：
- 现金低于：
- 连续 [N] 周没有有效用户证据：
- 产品交付无法形成复用：
- 家庭/健康/关系成本超过：
- 支持系统断裂：
```

## Common Mistakes

- Do not call a gamble "practice" if failure destroys the user's ability to continue.
- Do not use expected upside to justify ruin risk.
- Do not let people without consequences define success metrics for those who bear the cost.
- Do not add more process when stopping a harmful process would solve the problem.
- Do not treat past stability as proof of future safety when hidden tail risk exists.
