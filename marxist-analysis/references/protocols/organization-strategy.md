# Organization Strategy Protocol

Use this protocol for team conflict, party/organization questions, movement strategy, leadership, coordination, and route disputes.

## Required References

- Load `references/themes/dialectics-contradiction.md` for main-contradiction diagnosis.
- Load `references/themes/class-state-party.md` for organization and power-position mapping.
- Load `references/figures/mao.md` when the answer uses main contradiction, practice testing, or mass-line style investigation.
- Load `references/figures/lenin.md` only when party form, discipline, state power, or revolutionary organization is directly relevant.

## Steps

1. Identify the organization form: firm, team, party, movement, family, community, network.
2. Identify the main contradiction: line, resource, authority, legitimacy, capacity, or external pressure.
3. Identify actors and positions.
4. Separate antagonistic from non-antagonistic contradictions.
5. Identify the coordination mechanism that can actually change behavior.
6. Define a small organizational experiment.

## Questions

- Who decides?
- Who executes?
- Who blocks?
- Who has direct experience?
- Who bears the cost of wrong decisions?
- Is the conflict about goals, methods, resources, or trust?
